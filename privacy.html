Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

InkMirror (“the App”, “we”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • User Images: The photos you upload are used only to generate tattoo previews.
  • Generated Images: Generated tattoo images are shown to you in-app and are not shared.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized analytics (such as device type, session length) to improve the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core functionality of generating tattoo previews.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • To manage in-app purchases and credits.

Data Retention

  • Uploaded images are processed securely and deleted shortly after generation.
  • We do not permanently store your photos or tattoo images on our servers.

Third Parties

  • We may use trusted third-party services (e.g., Apple, Google, payment providers, AI APIs) to operate the app.
  • These providers have access only to the data necessary to perform their services.

Your Rights

  • You may delete the app at any time. We do not retain your images.
  • For questions, please contact us.
